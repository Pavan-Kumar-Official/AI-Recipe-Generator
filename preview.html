Tomato Basil Pasta

A fresh and flavorful pasta dish featuring ripe tomatoes, fragrant basil, and perfectly cooked pasta.

Prep

15 minutes

Cook

20 minutes

Serves

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti pasta
  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, torn
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add diced tomatoes and cook for 3-4 minutes until they begin to soften.
  6. Drain pasta and add to the skillet with tomato mixture.
  7. Toss with fresh basil, salt, and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
